What is KLX Digital?

About KLX Digital

KLX DIGITAL ($KLX) is a Real World Asset (RWA) token built on Ethereum (ERC-20). It represents 5% of the book value of PT Kristalin Eka Lestari (KEL), a licensed gold mining company in Indonesia. By bridging Indonesian gold into blockchain-based assets, KLX Digital provides global investors with transparent, verifiable, and borderless access to Indonesia's natural wealth.

  • Issuer: Kristalin Digital FZ-LLC (UAE)
  • Backing Entity: PT Kristalin Eka Lestari (Indonesia)
  • Total Supply: 200,000,000 $KLX
  • Asset Backing: 5% book value of Indonesian gold mining

Vision

To become the pioneer of Indonesian gold tokenization recognized globally as a transparent, regulated, and accessible Real World Asset.

Mission

  • Provide secure, transparent, and liquid blockchain-based investment instruments.
  • Bridge Indonesia's natural resources to the global digital market.
  • Ensure legal and regulatory compliance through structured issuance.
  • Promote blockchain adoption in mining and global investment sectors.
